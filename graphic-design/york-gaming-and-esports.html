Overview

York University’s Hub for esports, gaming, and club events. Welcoming everyone interested in all levels in gaming. York Gaming & Esports (YGE) is also home to esports teams including Valorant, CS:GO, League of Legends and more!

Being a volunteer and essentially working with the Club to generate these graphics in tandem with it’s club events and year of regaining footing from a post-covid decline in participation, it was very gratifying to interact with the community as well as create graphics that gave back and helped with the facilitation of the organization of the club!
