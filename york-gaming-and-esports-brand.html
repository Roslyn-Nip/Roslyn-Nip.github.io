Developing an existing brand while epowering the bold and friendly messaging during a period of regrowth for a gaming and esports club
Through working with club executives I was able to create refreshed  graphics and branding in to promote club events and recover from a post-covid decline in participation. In this process I managed a design team, created a simple brand guide as well as worked with the club’s frontend developer to create the first iteration of the club’s website.
